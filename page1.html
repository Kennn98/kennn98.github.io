Scene 1: HDI vs GNI overview.

The first scene is a scatterplot. In this scene, the x axis is HDI, and y-axis is GNI. The data are presented as circles, with color to be a HDI color scale consists for all charts, helping the user to understand the level of development intuitively. The location of the circle and the general trend can easily lead the viewer to develop a sense that the GNI is mostly positively correlated with HDI. There is button to invite the user to go to the next scene to dive deeper into the data.
